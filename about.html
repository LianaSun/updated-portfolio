About Liana Morrison


Hi! I'm Liana Morrison and I'm a Full-Stack Developer, Graphic Designer, MBA, Yoga & Dance Teacher, founder of The TITANESS Project, co-founder of The Minnesota Dancers Collective, mom, animal rights activist and healer. I have a passion for working with small businesses and entreprenures and helping them find their voice through graphic and web design. I look for the unique and beautiful in every day life and am constantly looking for new ways to challenge myself in order to grow and develop a a human being. I'm a solo-parent to 5 year old Amelia and we live in St. Paul with our corgi-mix, Penny and our Western Hognose Snake, Magical.
